Cong is a Visiting Assistant Professor of Mathematics at the University of Mississippi. Her research explores the interplay between free probability thoery, R-diagonal operator and random matrices. Specifically, she aims to understand and resolve the tension between the theory of noncommutative probability and infinite size random matrices that governs its eventual real-world big data. Right now, she is thinking about R-diagonal operator and infinite dimensional covariance matrices.

She earned her PhD in the Mathematics at Indiana University Bloomington, advised by Hari Bercovici.
